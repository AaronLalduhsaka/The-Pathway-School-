Academic Excellennce

Our school provides an education that goes beyond grades to develop all-around academic excellence – equipping young people with the confidence and resourcefulness that prepares them to grow, thrive, and find their success in a fast-changing world.

    I. Examinations
    1. Preparatory to KG: Two Term Tests (Mid-Term and Term-End).

    2. Classes 1 and above: Six Assessments are conducted:
  • Formative Assessments: 4 (FA1, FA2, FA3 and FA4).
    (FA1 and FA3 will be based solely on paper-pencil tests)
  • Summative Assessments: 2 (SA1 and SA2) If there is a bandh
    during the examination,a new date for that subject will be
    notified to the parents.

    • *The routine for other days will remain unchanged.

    II. Progress Report
    Progress Cards and Files containing the Assessed Answer
    Scripts will be given out to parents during the ‘Open House’.
    They must be returned to the Class Teacher on the next
    working day.

    III. Open House
    Open House will be held 2 times in a year for Preparatory to
    Kindergarten, and 4 times for Class 1 and above. Parents are
    requested to collect their child’s Progress Card and interact
    with the teachers during the Open House period.
