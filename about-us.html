VISION, MISSION & VALUES

  • OUR VISION

    • Our vision is to build and nurture a learning community who are committed to excellence.

  • OUR MISSION

    • Our mission is to provide an environment in which all students learn to the best of their potential. We will consistently strive for educational excellence.

  • OUR CORE VALUES

    • The Pathway School is committed to practise and teach our core values denoted by the acronym FRIEND to the best of our ability.

    1. Faith: We have faith in God and strongly believe
      that He would guide us in our path.
    2. Respect: We value ourselves and treat others and
      the environment with consideration and dignity.
    3. Integrity : We value honesty and truthfulness.
    4. Excellence: We hold ourselves and our students
      to high standards of performance and behaviour.
    5. Networking: We believe in collaboration. We will
      interact with our parents and others for their inputs.
    6. Diversity : We embrace diversity and value every
      student. We appreciate and learn from diverse
      viewpoints.
